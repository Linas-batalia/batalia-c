📋 Notes

  • HP - hit points. When it reaches 0, the unit dies.
  • AP - action points. Used for movement and actions.
  • Melee (atk+counter) - attack damage + counter-attack damage. "-" = cannot melee.
  • Ranged (dmg, range) - shot damage and range in hexes. "1-2" = 1 normal, 2 on backstab. "-" = cannot shoot.
  • Created From - defines unit progression tree. Format: "BaseUnit +item". "-" = base/starting unit.
  • Assigned Features - features the unit has from the start. Click "+" to add.
  • If a unit has Ranged data, it acts as an archer (can shoot). If it has Melee data, it can melee attack.

📋 Terrain Rules Summary (Demo Mode)

  • Movement Cost - multiplier applied to AP cost when entering terrain. 1.0 = normal, 2.0 = double cost, ∞ = impassable.
  • Arrow Protection - "Distance > 1" means arrows deal no damage if shooter is more than 1 hex away.
  • Combat Lock - "No lock" means the attacker does not engage/lock the defender on hills terrain.
  • Attack Phases - "1 only*" means attacker only deals damage in phase 1 unless they have Mountaineer feature.
  • Mountaineer - new feature required to deal full damage (phases 1 and 3) when attacking units on hills.
  • Knight Charge - only works on/into plains terrain. Cannot charge in forests or on hills.
  • Archer in Forest - maximum shooting range reduced to 1 hex.
  • Line of Sight - forests block LOS for ranged attacks. Hills do not block LOS.
Terrain modifiers affect Demo mode only. Classic and Arcade modes use standard rules.
