★ General

Any unit can be a General:

• +2 HP bonus

• ★ star and golden glow

• If defeated - game ends

🎯 Actions

Movement: 1 AP per hex

ZoC: 2 AP to leave enemy zone of control

Rotation: 1 free, others cost 1 AP

Attack: Consumes all AP

Shooting: 1 AP, up to 3 hexes

Castling: Swap (1 AP) or overstep (2 AP) friendly units

⚔ Combat

Attacker strikes first

Defender counter-strikes (1 damage)

Backstab: +1 damage from behind

Charge: +1 damage on first strike (cavalry on plains)

⛰ Hillbrace

When attacked from lowland to hill:

• Attacker does not lock defender

• Defender gets +1 damage on counter

• Archers on hills deal 1 damage in melee

• Shooting uphill: -1 range

🔒 Locking

After combat both units are locked

Defend: You strike first, disengage after. Stay in your hex.

Counterattack: You strike first (1 AP). Remain locked, pursue if enemy dies.

Retreat: Drag to adjacent hex (not in attacker's ZoC) - costs 1 AP & 1 HP, enemy takes your position

🛡 Shieldman

Regroup (1 AP): Heal +1 HP (max 3 uses)

Shield (1 AP): Block first arrow from each archer. Second arrow hits.

💡 Tips

• Protect your ★ General!

• Knights - best for offense

• Shieldmen - best for defense

• Archers - attack from behind

• Watch facing - avoid backstabs!
